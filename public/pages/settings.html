DMCA

This website respects the digital property rights of other and complies with the DMCA Act and other copyright laws. All media assets and game files are sourced from public third party websites and platforms.

If you found content on this website that you would like taken down, please send an email to me@x8r.dev and fill out the following:

  • Do you own the content?
  • Is the content copyrighted/licensed?
  • What is the name of the content?
  • Where is the content located? (URLs)
  • Do you have authorization to act on behalf of whoever/whatever owns this content?

Finally, please include contact information such as email or phone numbers.

We take DMCA requests seriously, and will make a strong attempt to respond and dispute as fast as possible.

If your DMCA takedown request is fake or a joke, it will be blocked and ignored.
